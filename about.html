About DVM

DVM was founded by Peng Zhao and Xu Wang in 2014 with the goal to Make VM run like Container.

With the rise of Docker, the public cloud of tomorrow will be very different than the cloud of today. And we believe that the future of Container-as-a-Service is just around the corner.

Here at DVM, we're developing technologies to Enable the public Container-as-a-Service. Our idea is to combine the best from both virtualization and container:

  • Fast as Container
  • Isolated by VM

We build software to take you all the way safely from today to tomorrow and beyond, and we are proud to be born and grow as an Open Source company, since 2014, from Beijing, China.
