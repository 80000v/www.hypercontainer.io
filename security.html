Security

We understand that many users place a high level of trust in tools we make to deploy and run their applications. We make the privacy of customer data and security of our tools and services a top priority. We apply best practices and focus on security to make sure we can maintain the trust of our customers.

Privacy

Each DVM product provides a published privacy policy that discloses what information may be collected and how it is used. Please see our individual products for their specific policy. For any specific questions, please contact security@getdvm.com.

Vulnerability Reporting

We deeply appreciate any effort to disclose responsibly.

If you would like to report a vulnerability, or have any security concerns with a DVM product, please e-mail security@getdvm.com. For non-critical matters, we prefer customers open a ticket with the appropriate product.
